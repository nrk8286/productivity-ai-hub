# Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Plans (Primary Revenue)

#### Free Tier
- **Price:** $0/month
- **Purpose:** Lead generation and user acquisition
- **Limits:** 
  - Up to 3 team members
  - 5 GB storage
  - Basic integrations
  - 7-day history
- **Conversion Strategy:** 
  - Prominently display upgrade prompts
  - Show what they're missing
  - Time-limited pro features trial

#### Professional Plan
- **Price:** $29/month ($290/year - save 17%)
- **Target:** Small to medium teams (5-25 people)
- **Features:**
  - Up to 25 team members
  - 100 GB storage
  - All integrations
  - Unlimited history
  - AI-powered insights
  - Priority support
- **Conversion Rate Target:** 3-5% of free users

#### Enterprise Plan
- **Price:** Custom (typically $500-2000/month)
- **Target:** Large organizations (25+ people)
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited team members
  - Unlimited storage
  - Custom integrations
  - Dedicated support
  - SLA guarantees
  - Custom AI models
  - White-label options
- **Sales Strategy:** Direct sales team

#### Expected Revenue

## Optimization Strategies

### Increase Free to Paid Conversion

1. **Time-Limited Trials**
   - 14-day pro feature trial
   - Automated email reminders
   - Usage-based triggers

2. **Usage-Based Upgrades**
   - Hit storage limit → upgrade prompt
   - Team size limit → upgrade prompt
   - Feature usage → "upgrade to keep using"

3. **Social Proof**
   - Testimonials on pricing page
   - Case studies
   - User count badges
   - "Join 2,000+ teams" messaging

4. **Value Demonstration**
   - ROI calculator
   - Time saved calculator
   - Before/after comparisons

### Reduce Churn

1. **Engagement Monitoring**
   - Track login frequency
   - Feature usage patterns
   - Proactive outreach for at-risk users

2. **Exit Surveys**
   - Understand why users leave
   - Offer discounts or alternatives
   - Collect feedback for improvements

3. **Value Reinforcement**
   - Weekly usage reports
   - Monthly ROI summaries
   - Feature discovery emails

### Increase ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)

1. **Upselling**
   - Professional → Enterprise
   - Monthly → Annual (17% discount)
   - Add-on features

2. **Cross-Selling**
   - Template packs
   - Training courses
   - Consulting services

3. **Volume Discounts**
   - Team size upgrades
   - Multi-year contracts
